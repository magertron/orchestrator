Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Magertron ("we," "us") handles information collected through our marketing website at magertron.com (the "Site"). It covers the Site only — not the Magertron product or platform, which is governed separately by your customer agreement.

Information we collect

When you visit the Site, standard technical information may be collected automatically, such as your IP address, browser type, device information, referring page, and the pages you view. If you contact us or request a demo, we collect the information you provide, such as your name, email, company, and message.

Analytics and visitor identification

With your consent, we use a third-party analytics and visitor-identification service (Leadsy, via the "vtag" tag loaded from r2.leadsy.ai) to understand Site traffic, measure interest, and support our business-to-business outreach. This tag may set cookies or use similar technologies and may associate Site activity with business contact information.

This tag loads only if you accept it via our cookie consent banner. If you decline, the tag is not loaded and this analytics collection does not occur. You can change your choice by clearing your browser's site data for magertron.com, which will cause the consent banner to appear again on your next visit.

How we use information

We use the information described above to operate and improve the Site, understand which content is useful, respond to inquiries and demo requests, and conduct business-to-business marketing and outreach. We do not sell personal information.

Sharing

We share information with service providers who help us run the Site and our outreach (such as the analytics provider named above and our email/CRM tools), only as needed to provide those services. We may also disclose information where required by law.

Your choices

You can decline analytics via the consent banner. You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you, or to opt out of certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

Data retention

We retain information for as long as needed for the purposes described here or as required by law, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

International visitors

We are based in the United States and process information there. If you visit from outside the U.S., you consent to that processing where you accept analytics or otherwise submit information to us.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your information: privacy@magertron.com.
