Showback & chargeback
One lump bill becomes
a governed, forecastable line
An invoice-style chargeback statement that attributes spend down to the team, the cost center, and the individual caller — human user or service-account agent.

Spend, attributed to who spent it.

Per-vendor statements roll up by department and cost center, then break down to each caller with their share of the total. Value-based dollars computed from billable calls at the contract rate.

  • Department → caller hierarchy with cost-center codes and per-line percentages.
  • User and service-account attribution — every agent identified.
  • Per-vendor scoping so each statement foots to one contract.
  • Export to PDF — a finance-ready document, not a raw export.
